Who We Are

enLight10 Solutions is a veteran-owned, DoD-focused Azure security firm. We help defense contractors protect Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI), operationalize Zero Trust, and stay audit-ready on Microsoft GCC High using the tools they already own — Purview, Sentinel, Defender, and Entra.

Our Mission

Bring clarity and confidence to the Defense Industrial Base by turning complex compliance into practical, automated safeguards — so teams can move faster without risking contracts or data.

What We Do

We design, harden, and operate Microsoft 365 GCC High environments for DoD contractors — aligning security controls with CMMC, NIST 800-171/172, DFARS, DoDI 5200.48, and Zero Trust guidance. Our work blends hands-on engineering with evidence-ready documentation.

  • GCC High expertise: Purview (CUI labeling), Sentinel (SIEM), Defender (EDR), Entra (IAM).
  • Compliance first: control mapping, evidence capture, and audit prep built into operations.
  • Operational pragmatism: guardrails that fit real users, devices, and missions.

Who We Are

Built by operators. Focused on mission outcomes.

enLight10 Solutions is a veteran-owned cybersecurity company focused on supporting government and defense operations — and building Watchtower, a SOC platform shaped by real-world experience.

We operate at the intersection of practitioner reality, mission constraints, and modern technology. The work we do — and the platform we’re building — comes from lived experience inside cyber teams responsible for critical infrastructure, defense networks, and national-level missions.

Founder

enLight10 Solutions was founded by Jay Moreno, a cybersecurity operator and leader with over 15 years of technical experience and 9 years dedicated to defensive cyber operations across military, federal, and government contracting environments.

  • U.S. Air Force (Active Duty): early career in network operations and enterprise command-and-control support.
  • U.S. Army National Guard & Deployment: systems administration, IT modernization, and deployed communications support across multiple theaters.
  • Cyber Protection Team (CPT): selected as one of the top cybersecurity operators across the Guard (~20,000 personnel).
  • USCYBERCOM–Aligned Missions: threat hunter supporting Cyber National Mission Teams and hunt-forward operations.
  • GG-13 Cyber Hunt Lead: led advanced hunt operations with NSA Texas and international deployments supporting U.S. and allied defense partners.
  • SOC & Ops Leadership: directed security operations, IR, threat hunting, automation efforts, and security modernization programs.

Jay’s operational focus — from CPT missions and hunt operations to enterprise SOC leadership — directly informs how Watchtower is being built and how we support customers today.

Why We’re Building Watchtower

Watchtower exists to change that — giving analysts one place to investigate, document, and communicate clearly.

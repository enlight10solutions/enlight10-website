What Watchtower Does

Unified Analyst Workspace

Alerts, cases, context, and analyst findings live in one interface — not buried in multiple tools or chat threads.

AI-Assisted Investigation

AI helps analyze alerts, summarize investigations, draft timelines, and create defensible documentation.

Operational Workflow Support

Built for shift work, handoffs, escalation paths, and government-style reporting formats.

Who It's For

Watchtower is designed for:

  • Government SOC teams and cybersecurity offices
  • Defense contractors with monitoring and investigation needs
  • Organizations maturing from tickets + spreadsheets toward repeatable processes

What Watchtower Does

Unified Analyst Workspace

Alerts, cases, context, and analyst findings live in one interface — not buried in multiple tools or chat threads.

AI-Assisted Investigation

AI helps analyze alerts, summarize investigations, draft timelines, and create defensible documentation that leadership and auditors can actually understand.

Operational Workflow Support

Built for shift work, handoffs, escalation paths, and government-style reporting formats — but simple enough for small security teams to adopt.

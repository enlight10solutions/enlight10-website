Watchtower CRP™

The Cyber Reasoning Platform that transforms your existing security tools into organized understanding, actionable response, and continuous assurance for SecureOps and Cyber Defense United.

What Watchtower CRP Does

Unifies Signals into Stories

Watchtower CRP ingests alerts, logs, and context from your SIEM, EDR, identity, and network tools and groups them into WCS-backed buckets that represent behaviors and campaigns, not just single events.

Builds Evidence as You Work

Every investigation becomes a case with timelines, artifacts, decisions, and outcomes — ready for command briefings, auditors, and CMMC/cATO evidence without extra paperwork.

Supports Real-World Operations

Designed for shift work, escalation paths, and handoffs — so NSOCs, CSSPs, MSSPs, and small teams can all operate in a consistent, repeatable way across Watchtower SecureOps and Cyber Defense United.

Core Modules in Watchtower CRP

The platform is built from focused modules that work together — so you can start where you are and grow into full SecureOps and Cyber Defense United operations.

Guardian — Alerts & Triage

Normalizes alerts from SIEM and tools into a consistent view, applies WCS-driven correlations, and feeds prioritized buckets into your queues. Less swivel-chair, more “here’s what matters.”

Seek — WCS Bucket Engine

The reasoning core: Watchtower Common Schema (WCS) fused with correlation logic to group related events into buckets that represent behaviors, attack paths, and anomalies.

Atlas — Investigations & Timelines

Hands-on investigation space where analysts manage evidence, timelines, notes, and conclusions. Perfect for IR, hunt, and long-running mission cases.

Assist — AI Copilots

AI copilots for SOC analysts and leadership that summarize cases, propose next steps, draft reports, and help translate technical details into commander-ready language — with humans always in control.

Deploy — Automation & SOAR

Connects Watchtower CRP to SOAR tools like Tines and your existing automation stack to orchestrate enrichment, containment, health checks, and ticket updates.

Audit — Evidence & Compliance

Aligns cases, actions, and artifacts to controls — turning day-to-day operations into direct evidence for CMMC, RMF, and cATO without bolt-on tooling.

Future modules include Replay (re-simulating incidents), Intel (fusing threat intelligence), Graph (entity relationships), and Training (scenario-driven exercises) — all building on the same CRP core.

Who Watchtower CRP Is For

The CRP is designed for teams that need reasoning, not just more alerts:

  • Defense & Government NSOCs / CSSPs that must brief leadership, support cATO, and operate across boundary, enclave, and mission networks.
  • Defense contractors who need monitoring, investigation, and CMMC-aligned evidence without building a platform from scratch.
  • MSSPs and SOC providers that want a repeatable operating model to onboard tenants, standardize workflows, and prove value.
  • Security-focused SMBs that need structure, timelines, and documentation — not just raw logs and tickets.

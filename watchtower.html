Watchtower

Watchtower is an emerging Security Operations Platform built for government, defense, and security-focused small businesses that need a clearer way to investigate, document, and communicate what’s happening in their environment.

Instead of bouncing between tickets, dashboards, and chat threads, Watchtower gives analysts one place to work — with AI assistance that helps them move faster, not lose control.

Request Early Access Follow Development

What Watchtower Does

Unified Analyst Workspace

Alerts, cases, context, and analyst findings live in one interface — not buried in multiple tools or chat threads.

AI-Assisted Investigation

AI helps analyze alerts, summarize investigations, draft timelines, and create defensible documentation that leadership and auditors can actually understand.

Operational Workflow Support

Built for shift work, handoffs, escalation paths, and government-style reporting formats — but simple enough for small security teams to adopt.

Who It's For

Watchtower is designed for:

  • Government SOC teams and cybersecurity offices
  • Defense contractors with monitoring and investigation needs
  • Security teams at small and mid-size businesses that need structure without a huge tooling stack
  • Organizations maturing from tickets + spreadsheets toward repeatable, auditable processes

Pilot Program

Watchtower is currently available through a limited early access pilot. Pilot participants help validate workflows, shape features, and provide operational feedback in exchange for early capability and direct engineering involvement.

  • Hands-on onboarding and guidance
  • Access to emerging features
  • Direct input to the roadmap

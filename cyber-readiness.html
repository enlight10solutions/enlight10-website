What the Review Covers

The review is scoped around your goals, environment, compliance drivers, and available evidence.

Assessment Scope

  • Business and mission context
  • Key systems and critical services
  • Security posture and control gaps
  • Policy, incident response plan, and evidence review

Mapping and Improvement

  • Compliance mapping to NIST, CMMC, or SOC 2 where applicable

  • SOC, detection, and response workflow opportunities

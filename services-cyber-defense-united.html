What Cyber Defense United Delivers

Cyber Defense United brings together two things most programs struggle to unify: the Cyber Reasoning Platform (Watchtower CRP) and experienced defenders who can triage, investigate, and respond. We operate as a DoD/DIB-aligned MSSP, an on-prem tool integrator, and a contracting partner for IT/cyber programs that need measurable outcomes and defensible evidence.

Managed Detection & Response (MDR/MSSP)

Ongoing monitoring, triage, investigation, and incident response — designed for high-consequence environments where you must prove what happened and what you did about it.

  • 24/7 or mission-aligned coverage models
  • Case-based investigations with timelines and artifacts
  • IR support: containment guidance, recovery support, after-action reporting

Watchtower CRP as the Reasoning Core

Watchtower CRP acts as the operational brain that unifies alerts, context, and evidence across tools — so decisions are faster, clearer, and easier to defend.

  • Unified investigations across SIEM/EDR/identity/network sources
  • Evidence packs: “here’s what we saw → what it meant → what we did”
  • Standardized playbooks and reporting across enclaves/tenants

On-Prem Integration & Tool Rationalization

We deploy and integrate in the environments you actually have: on-prem, constrained, or air-gapped. We align your tool stack to operations and reduce chaos, not add more dashboards.

  • SIEM/EDR/log pipeline integrations and tuning
  • On-prem deployments & disconnected operations patterns
  • Modernization plans grounded in “battle rhythm” and reporting

cATO Enablement, Automation, and AI Integration

Many “cATO” efforts fail because they treat operations as paperwork. We focus on the parts that make cATO real: continuous monitoring, repeatable operations, and evidence. Cyber Defense United can be paired with an automation toolset and AI integration support so your program improves security outcomes while reducing manual burden.

Operational cATO Package

A packaged approach that combines Watchtower-led monitoring with supporting tooling to automate hardening, checks, and evidence capture — built to support A&A realities and continuous monitoring expectations.

  • Continuous monitoring workflows + reporting cadence
  • Automated evidence generation aligned to operations (cases/timelines/artifacts)
  • Integration of supporting automation tools (per enclave constraints)

AI Integration Support (Human-in-the-Loop)

We integrate AI where it actually helps defenders: summarization, triage assistance, report drafting, and decision support — with analyst control and auditable outputs.

  • Assist-driven summaries and “next-step” suggestions
  • Report and briefing acceleration for leadership and auditors
  • AI governance patterns: logging, review gates, and traceability

DoD Contracting Support for IT & Cyber Programs

In addition to managed defense, Cyber Defense United supports DoD contracting needs across IT and cyber: modernization, operations design, engineering support, and mission-ready reporting. If you need a partner that can deliver both operations and technical execution, we can align to your program structure and constraints.

SOC / NSOC Modernization

Tiering models, escalation paths, shift design, battle rhythm, and reporting — aligned to your mission and resourcing realities.

Engineering + Integration

Build the backbone: pipelines, integrations, tuning, and operational dashboards — with Watchtower as the unifying workspace.

Compliance-Driven Operations

Evidence you can defend: cases, timelines, artifacts, and repeatable workflows that support CMMC and continuous monitoring expectations.

What we deliver

Assessment through operations, built for mission requirements.

CDU combines readiness assessments, compliance support, and Watchtower-powered evidence organization into a delivery model that fits how DoD and DIB programs actually work.

CMMC & NIST Readiness

Gap assessment against NIST 800-171 and CMMC controls, with practical remediation plans you can act on — not just a findings list.

  • Control gap mapping (NIST 800-171 / CMMC Level 2)
  • SSP development support
  • POA&M creation and structured tracking

Mission Cyber Risk

Translate technical vulnerabilities and control gaps into mission risk — framed for program leads, primes, and leadership rather than technical analysts.

  • Mission impact framing for technical findings
  • Risk prioritization aligned to program realities
  • Risk acceptance and deferral documentation

Evidence & Defensible Records

Build and maintain the documentation trail that assessors, auditors, contracting officers, and oversight teams expect to see.

  • Organized evidence tied to controls and findings
  • Assessment and audit-ready reporting packages
  • Prime-sub reporting support and subcontract delivery

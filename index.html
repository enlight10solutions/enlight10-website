What Is a Cyber Reasoning Platform?

Traditional tools generate alerts. Analysts chase noise. Compliance drains time. A Cyber Reasoning Platform changes the model by focusing on behavior, intent, and the evidence your commanders, CISOs, and auditors need.

Understands Behavior

Watchtower CRP ingests signals from your existing tools and focuses on intent and impact — not just signatures or single events.

Explains in Plain Language

Each investigation includes narrative + timeline + supporting evidence, so leaders and auditors see the same story as analysts.

Drives Action

Reasoning, risk, and recommended actions are surfaced together — moving you from “something happened” to “here’s what we’re doing about it.”

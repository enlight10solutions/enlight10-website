What Is a Cyber Reasoning Platform?

Traditional tools generate alerts. Analysts chase noise. Compliance drains time. A Cyber Reasoning Platform changes the model by focusing on behavior, intent, and the evidence your commanders, CISOs, and auditors need.

Understands Behavior

Watchtower CRP ingests signals from your existing tools and focuses on intent and impact — not just signatures or single events. It builds correlated buckets of activity and shows you why they matter.

Explains in Plain Language

Each investigation comes with a narrative, timeline, and supporting evidence, so leadership, analysts, and auditors see the same story — not just a wall of alerts and tickets.

Drives Action, Not Just Alerts

Reasoning, risk, and recommended actions are surfaced together — so you can move from “something happened” to “here’s what we’re doing about it,” with documentation built in.

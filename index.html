Assess. Improve. Prove.

enLight10 starts with the environment in front of you: business context, systems, compliance drivers, policies, evidence, risk goals, and operating constraints. Then we map risk, prioritize action, and help prove progress.

1. Intake

Capture business and mission context, key systems, compliance drivers, current policies, available evidence, and the risk questions leadership needs answered.

2. Assess

Review security posture, control gaps, exposure, process maturity, evidence quality, SOC workflows, and OT/ICS readiness where applicable.

3. Map

Map findings to NIST CSF, NIST 800-53, NIST 800-171, CMMC, SOC 2, evidence gaps, risk register entries, and remediation needs where applicable.

4. Prioritize

Identify quick wins, high-risk fixes, owners, sequencing, and the roadmap needed to move from findings to measurable improvement.

5. Improve

Support SOC uplift, remediation tracking, playbooks, reporting, and Watchtower workflows that help teams maintain proof of progress.
